# Bolt of Ophiotaurus

***A.I. Description***
```
This is 1 of 100.

This item acts as a regular crossbow bolt unless it is shot directly into the eye of a deity. Upon a successful strike to the god’s eye, the deity’s invulnerability pauses for fifteen seconds.
```


Type: Ammunition 
Cost: 1 gp 
Weight: 1 1/2 lb
